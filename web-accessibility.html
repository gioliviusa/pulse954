ADA & WCAG Compliant Accessibility Features

Professional website accessibility tools for hotels and restaurants

Font Size Control

Let guests adjust text size from 80% to 150% for comfortable reading. Perfect for older guests or those with visual impairments viewing your menu, room descriptions, and booking information.

  • Easy-to-use size slider
  • Settings saved automatically
  • Works across all pages
  • Smooth scaling transitions

High Contrast Mode

Maximize visibility for guests with low vision or color blindness. High contrast mode makes text, buttons, and images stand out for easier navigation and booking.

  • Enhanced color contrast
  • Better visibility for CTAs
  • Accessible form fields
  • Instant toggle on/off

Dyslexia-Friendly Font

Switch to OpenDyslexic font designed specifically for guests with dyslexia. Weighted letter bottoms prevent swapping and improve reading comprehension of your content.

  • Specialized readable font
  • Reduces reading errors
  • One-click activation
  • Improves comprehension
